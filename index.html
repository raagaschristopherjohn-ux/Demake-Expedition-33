EXPEDITION 33

— A DEMAKE GAUNTLET —

Lead Gustave, Lune, Maelle & Sciel through 33 levels to the Paintress. Each round a random turn order decides who acts (so you can't lean on one hero), and every hero has their own AP to spend on their three signature skills. Press Enter or END HERO to pass to the next.

Defense is active. When a hero is struck, shrinking rings race toward the gold ring — tap SPACE as each aligns: PERFECT PARRY (0 damage + counter + charge), GRAZE DODGE, or a MISS. Missing only part of a combo costs only part of the damage.

Difficulty scales by how many rings you must parry and how fast they shrink — comfortably readable, never frame-perfect.
